PROJECT OVERVIEW:-

This project, Vityarthi digital literacy, focuses on building essential digitalskills required for academic, preofessional, and personal growth. It covers practical knowledge of digital tools, online platforms, cybersecuity awareness, and professional communication. 

This project includes hands-on task such as creating online profiles, using collaborative tools, designing forms, understanding cybercrimes, and maintaining a Github portfolio. The aim is to develop confidence in using modern digital technologies and responsibility.
The course emphasizes real-world application through assignments, promoting skills like problem solving, online safety, digital communication, and content creation.

MODULE SUMMARIES-

**MODULE-1**-Introduction to digital literacy and Presentation tools:
This module introduces the concept of digital literacy and its importance in today's world. It covers tools like Canva, Prezi, and Meero to create presentations, designs, visual content. Students learn how to effectively communicate ideas using digital platforms.

**MODULE-2**- Building a digital Portfolio:
This module focuses on creating and managing proessional online profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle. It helps students build an online presence and showcase their sklills and work.

**MODULE-3**- Programming and collabraton programs:
Students explore collaborative tools such as Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Forms. It includes coding platforms like HackerRank, CodeChef, and HackerEarth to improve problem solving and programming skills. 

**MODULE-4**- Email and Social media Etiquette:
This module teaches professional communication, including writing formal emails and maintaining proper behaviour on social media. It highlights the importance of positive and responsible digital presence. 

**MODULE-5**- Cybercrime Awareness and Safety:
This module explains different types of cybercrimes such as Phishing, Fraud, Identity theft. It also provides preventation strategies and promotes safe online practices to protect personal and financial data.

Conclusion:
Overall, this project helps studets become digitally literate individuals who can safely and efficiently use technologies in real life scenarios.
