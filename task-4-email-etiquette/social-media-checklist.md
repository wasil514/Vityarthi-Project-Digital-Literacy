✅ Do’s
1. Think before posting anything online.
2. Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings.
3. Share accurate and verified information.
4. Respect others and communicate politely.
5. Maintain a positive and professional online presence.


❌ Don’ts
1. Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or offensive behavior
3. Don’t believe or spread fake news
4. Don’t overshare or post inappropriate content
5. Don’t click on suspicious links or messages
