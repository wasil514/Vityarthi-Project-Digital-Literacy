STAY SAFE ONLINE- CHECKLIST

1. Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication(2FA).
2. Never share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bannk or official services.
3. Always verify links and emails before clicking; avoid suspicious or unknown sources.
4. Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts.
6. For UPI safety: Double check the receiver's name before sending money and confirm the UPI ID carefully.
7. For UPI safety: Never approve unknown payment requests or "collect requests" on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
8. Limit the personal information you share on social media and adjusr privacy settings regularly.
9. Regularly monitor your bank statemnets and transaction history for any suspicious activity.
